About Opet

Opet is a cute pixelart online pet that you can take care of.

Feed it, play with it, watch it level up and much more!

Are you ready to take care of some cute pets?

Tips

Opets like to eat and drink a lot, but watch out that you don't give them too much!

Sleep is the best way to get some energy in after a long cuddle session

Don't be shy, cuddle them by clicking on them!












Opets don't like to be disturbed after their bedtime

They mostly sleep after 10 PM till 8AM, so don't worry you have plenty of time to take care of them

Eating takes energy, so make sure to let them rest enough

Don't let them train and play over thier limits

If you damage your friendship a lot, then you gonna have to start from scratch again
